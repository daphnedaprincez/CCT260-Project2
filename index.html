And I'm a Big Dreamer

I'm a front-end software engineer with a passion for mixing code and creativity. As a second-year CCIT student at the University of Toronto, I spend my time building, designing, and exploring ideas that live somewhere between software and art.

I have a huge love for software and game development, digital storytelling, product and graphic design, and everything visual. I'm curious, a little bold, and always excited to learn, especially when it means turning ideas into meaningful things.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can usually find me drawing concept art, traveling, exploring the world of games, or creating little adventures on my TikTok and YouTube channels.

Thanks for stopping by, and welcome to my creative corner.
